Nusantara 2016

Brought to you by BISA, Nusantara is a half-day long cultural festival that aims to showcase the Indonesian culture. Hosted at Memorial Glade, there will be multiple food vendors selling authentic Indonesian food as well as fun games for all to play. Throughout the day, multiple groups - from traditional dance communities to Indonesian bands - will also perform for your viewing pleasure. We are excited to share our rich culture with you and we hope that you will be there to experience it!

Berkeley Indonesian Student Association presents:

Nusantara 2016

Memorial Glade


10:00 AM - 06:00 PM
